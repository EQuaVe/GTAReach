Compute next state.

Parameters
system: a system
vloc: tuple of locations
intval: valuation of bounded integer variables
vedge: tuple of edges
src_invariant: clock constraint container for invariant of vloc before it is updated
guard: clock constraint container for guard of vedge
reset: clock resets container for clock resets of vedge
tgt_invariant: clock constaint container for invariant of vloc after it is updated
edges: tuple of edge from vloc (range of synchronized/asynchronous edges)
Precondition
the source location in edges match the locations in vloc. No process has more than one edge in edges. The pid of every process in edges is less than the size of vloc
Postcondition
the locations in vloc have been updated to target locations of the processes involved in edges, and they have been left unchanged for the other processes. The values of variables in intval have been updated according to the statements in edges. Clock constraints from the invariants of vloc before it is updated have been pushed to src_invariant. Clock constraints from the guards in edges have been pushed into guard. Clock resets from the statements in edges have been pushed into reset. And clock constraints from the invariants in the updated vloc have been pushed into tgt_invariant
Returns
STATE_OK if state computation succeeded, STATE_INCOMPATIBLE_EDGE if the source locations in edges do not match vloc, STATE_SRC_INVARIANT_VIOLATED if the valuation intval does not satisfy the invariant in vloc, STATE_GUARD_VIOLATED if the values in intval do not satisfy the guard of edges, STATE_STATEMENT_FAILED if statements in edges cannot be applied to intval STATE_TGT_INVARIANT_VIOLATED if the updated intval does not satisfy the invariant of updated vloc.
Exceptions
std::invalid_argument: if a pid in edges is greater or equal to the size of vloc
std::runtime_error: if the guard in edges generates clock resets, or if the statements in edges generate clock constraints, or if the invariant in updated vloc generates clock resets
std::runtime_error: if evaluation of invariants, guards or statements throws an exception
