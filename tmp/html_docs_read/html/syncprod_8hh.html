Compute initial state.

Parameters
system: a system
vloc: tuple of locations
vedge: tuple of edges
initial_range: range of initial locations
Precondition
the size of vloc and vedge is equal to the size of initial_range. initial_range has been obtained from system. initial_range yields the initial locations of all the processes ordered by increasing process identifier
Postcondition
vloc has been initialized to the tuple of initial locations in initial_range. vedge has been initialized to an empty tuple of edges
Returns
tchecker::STATE_OK
Exceptions
std::invalid_argument: if the size of vloc, vedge and initial_range do not coincide

Compute next state.

Parameters
vloc: tuple of locations
vedge: tuple of edges
edges: range of edges in a asynchronous/synchronized edge from vloc
Precondition
the source locations of edges match the locations in vloc, no process has more than one edge in vedge, and the pid of every process in vedge is less than the size of vloc
Postcondition
the locations in vloc have been updated to target locations of edges for processes involved in edges. They and have been left unchanged for the other processes. vedge contains the identifiers of the edges in edges
Returns
tchecker::STATE_OK if the sources locations in edges match the locations in vloc, tchecker::STATE_INCOMPATIBLE_EDGE otherwise
Exceptions
std::invalid_argument: if the sizes of vloc and vedge do not match, or if the pid of an edge in edges is greater or equal to the size of vloc/vedge
